About

Welcome to my page! I am Abdus Salam. I was born on August 20, 2006, in a serene and beautiful village in the Chandina Upazila of Cumilla. I am the son of Abdul Kader and Halema Begum. It is through their boundless love, care, and moral guidance that I have become the person I am today. My roots lie in the Kaduti Bazar area of Borkoroi Union, under Chandina Police Station. Since childhood, I have felt a deep, soulful connection to this land. I began my academic journey at Kaduti Government Primary School, where I successfully completed my 5th grade. Later, I enrolled in Kaduti High School for my secondary education and completed the 10th grade there.
