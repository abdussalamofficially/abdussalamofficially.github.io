About Me

Welcome to my page! I am Abdus Salam. I was born on August 20, 2006, in a serene and beautiful village in the Chandina Upazila of Cumilla. I am the son of Abdul Kader and Halema Begum. It is through their boundless love, care, and moral guidance that I have become the person I am today. My roots lie in the Kaduti Bazar area of Borkoroi Union, under Chandina Police Station. Since childhood, I have felt a deep, soulful connection to this land. I began my academic journey at Kaduti Government Primary School, where I successfully completed my 5th grade. Later, I enrolled in Kaduti High School for my secondary education and completed the 10th grade there. The foundation of my personality and thoughts was built amidst this rural environment and a simple way of life. Currently, I am navigating a new chapter of my life. After finishing my studies, I chose to move abroad to bring a smile to my family's face and to build a brighter future for myself. On March 4, 2025, I moved to the Maldives for work. Despite the busy life in a foreign land, I am striving as a confident young man to fulfill my dreams. My identity and my roots remain my greatest source of pride and inspiration.
